Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
Basic Standing Pose

The Mountain Pose is the foundation for all standing poses in yoga. It helps improve posture and balance. Stand tall, feet together, with arms by your sides, and focus on breathing deeply.

Downward Dog Pose
Inverted V Pose

Downward Dog is a foundational pose in yoga. It stretches the hamstrings, calves, and back, and improves strength and flexibility.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)
Powerful Standing Pose

Warrior Pose helps to build strength and stamina, enhancing balance and focus in both the body and mind.

Tree Pose (Vrksasana)
Balance Pose

Tree Pose helps improve balance and stability by grounding through the standing leg and engaging the core.

Child's Pose (Balasana)
Restorative Pose

Child's Pose is a resting pose that helps to stretch the hips, thighs, and ankles while calming the mind and relieving stress.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)
Backbend Pose

Cobra Pose strengthens the spine and opens the chest and shoulders, improving flexibility and posture.
